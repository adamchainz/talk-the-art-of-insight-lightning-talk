Problem 1.15) What is the Mass of the Earth?

Mass of Earth?

Volume of the Earth × Mass per m3 of the Earth

mass(earth)?

volume(earth) × m3_mass(earth)

volume(earth)?

4 × π × radius(earth)3

radius(earth)?

radius(earth)?

  • flight = 12 hours × 400km/hour
  • flight = 4,800km
  • circumference(earth) = 4.5 × flight
  • circumference(earth) = 4.5 × 4,800km
  • circumference(earth) = 21,600km
  • radius(earth) = 21,600km / π
  • radius(earth) = 6,875km

radius(earth)?

6,875km

volume(earth)?

4 × π × radius(earth)3

4 × π × (6,875km)3

4 × π × (6,875,000m)3

volume(earth)?

4.1 × 1021m3

mass(earth)?

volume(earth) × m3_mass(earth)

m3_mass(earth)?

m3_mass(earth)?

1,000 litres in m3

1 litre water = 1kg

1 litre rock = 2kg

m3_mass(rock) = 2,000kg

mass(earth)?

volume(earth) × m3_mass(earth)

mass(earth)?

4.1 × 1021 m3 × 2,000kg/m3

mass(earth)?

8 × 1024kg

mass(earth)?

8 × 1024kg

Real answer: 5.97237 × 1024kg
